How to Use the Calculator

Step-by-Step Guide

This guide will walk you through how to use the Alzheimer's Disease Risk Calculator.

  1. Enter Your Age: Type your age in the first box.
  2. Select Your Sex: Choose Male or Female from the dropdown menu.
  3. Cognitive Status: Select the option that best describes your memory or thinking concerns.
  4. APOE Status: If you know your APOE gene type, select it from the list. If you don't know, choose "I don't know".
  5. Test Result: Select your result from the pTau 217/β-Amyloid 1-42 Plasma Ratio test.
  6. Calculate Risk: Click the "Calculate Risk" button.

The results will show your risk level for developing Alzheimer's disease.
